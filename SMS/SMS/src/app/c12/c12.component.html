Chennai Public School, Anna Nagar

Chennai Public School in Anna Nagar is one of the most popular schools today. It has more than 4000 students and 200 faculty members and is promoted by the Kupidisaatham Narayanaswami Educational Trust (KNET). It also has a branch in Thirumazhisai. Both branches are famous for providing top-notch education to students along with facilities for activities and sports

Admissions

The academic year begins in April for UKG to Grade XII students and in June for LKG students. The admission forms are available at the campus and must be collected personally. Parents must complete the form and submit it within 3 working days with the required documents and the application fee. After this, the school will conduct an admission test that covers specific subjects based on the grade in which admission is being sought.
Admission is only granted to students who satisfy the age criteria and pass the admission test.
The school also offers merit-based scholarships to students of Grades XI and XII.

Facilities

AC classrooms, interactive digital learning, 3D sci-fi theater, transport, library, language labs, robotics lab, exclusive training in martial arts, yoga, archery, etc.
