Orchids The International School

Orchids The International School, one of the best schools in Chennai, is a top-notch international CBSE institution. The school admission is open for students in Early Childhood (from 3 years) to grade 5. Orchids at Thoraipakkam is one of the best CBSE schools in Chennai since it adheres to the highest international educational standards. The institution’s overarching goal is to unite students’ academic and extracurricular pursuits. The school provides a setting where kids may develop and hone their skills using state-of-the-art facilities (such as playgrounds, skating rinks, basketball courts, swimming pools, libraries, and activity rooms).

Facilities

The school offers a range of facilities to its students, including well-equipped classrooms, science and computer labs, a library, a playground, and a cafeteria that serves healthy meals.

Curriculum

The Early Years Program (EYP) forms the basis of the CBSE curriculum at this school, which provides a full range of English-language academic programs beginning in Early Childhood. One of the distinguishing features of this school is the Early Years Programme (EYP), which has made the school one of the top CBSE schools in Thoraipakkam by encouraging children to actively learn the fundamentals of their education. This is one of the top-rated schools in Thoraipakkam.
