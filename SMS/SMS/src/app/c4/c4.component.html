Maharishi Vidya Mandir

Maharishi Vidya Mandir Chetpet, one of the top schools in Chennai, first opened its doors in 1983, with Yoganas, Pranayama, and Transcendental Meditation being an essential element of its curriculum. There is a comprehensive response to raising students’ levels of awareness throughout all domains of education, including the classroom, extracurricular activities, cultural celebrations, service movements, sports, technology-enhanced education, and the cultivation of the student’s unique character. The school is affiliated to the CBSE board and is one of the best cbse schools in Chennai.

Facilities

The school offers a range of facilities to its students, including spacious classrooms, science and computer labs, a library, a playground, and a cafeteria that serves vegetarian meals.

Location

Maharishi Gardens, 28, Dr. Guruswamy Road, Chetpet, Chennai-600 031.

Curriculum

The school follows the curriculum pattern of C.B.S.E. Schools from STD. I – XII and the curriculum as prescribed by NCERT is followed for Class I to XII. The school is one of the finest schools in Chetpet.

Classes

Maharishi Vidya Mandir offers classes from pre-KG to Grade 12.
