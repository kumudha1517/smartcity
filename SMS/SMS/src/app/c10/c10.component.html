Abacus Montessori School

Abacus Montessori School is considered as one of the top schools in Chennai and is a modern Montessori institution that values a healthy mix of academic success, care for the individual student, and moral compass. The school is associated with the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations in New Delhi and serves about 400 students in grades elementary through twelve. This is one of the best icse schools in Chennai.

Physical Education

Sports like football, basketball, cricket, hockey, and all forms of track and field can be practiced in the Physical Education room. Indoor badminton, table tennis, chess, and carom sets are also available. Second graders on up to high schoolers make use of the facilities. In addition to its indoor facilities, Abacus also features an open auditorium, courtyard, playgrounds, and a small football field. It is one of the best schools in Perungudi.
