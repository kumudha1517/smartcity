Ryan International School

Ryan International School Sriperumbudur, one of the best schools in Chennai, which opened in 2013, is dedicated to providing an environment that fosters student’s academic development and personal growth. Respect, honesty, hard effort, self-discipline, justice, and care are just some of the qualities that are found here. This school, among other CBSE schools, is one of the best CBSE schools in Chennai.

Why choose RIS?

When it comes to elementary, middle, and high school education in India, this school stands head and shoulders above the rest. The goal of the academic programs offered by the Ryan group is to foster the development of lifelong learners, and these courses are all designed in accordance with the company’s 12 core values. The school is an international partnership between educational institutions with the goal of giving Ryan Group students access to educational opportunities all across the world. This is one of the best schools in Mambakkam.

Facilities

The school offers a range of facilities to its students, including well-equipped classrooms, science and computer labs, a library, a swimming pool, a basketball court, a football field, and a playground for younger children. The school also has a medical room and a cafeteria that serves healthy meals
