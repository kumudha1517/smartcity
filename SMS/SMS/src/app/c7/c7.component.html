Air Force School

The Air Force School was established in 1948 and is considered as one of the top schools in Chennai. The school is a Central Board of Secondary Education–recognized, coeducational institution (CBSE). The IAF Educational & Cultural Society is in charge of it. This is one of the best cbse schools in Chennai.
