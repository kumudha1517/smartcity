About Delhi Public School

Delhi Public School Uniworld City, one of the top schools in Chennai, was established by the Delhi Public School Society in 2013. The school provides education from kindergarten to tenth grade and is recognized by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) in New Delhi. Its original driving force was a commitment to making education accessible to all members of society.

Facilities

The school, one of the best cbse schools in Chennai, provides a variety of infrastructure amenities, including a library that boasts of an excellent collection of literature suitable for all levels and age groups. Science labs, Audio Visual Rooms that are heavily used to assist visual learning, and an Auditorium for students to use. DPS is one of the best schools in Nallambakkam for your child.
