Chettinad Vidyashram

Chettinad Vidyashram is the brainchild of Dr. Mrs. Meena Muthiah. She is also the current senior principal of the school. Chettinad was established with the mission to shape young minds through holistic development. Today, thanks to this vision and the faculty’s dedication, it is one of the best schools in Chennai.

Admissions

When admission season starts, the school publishes a notice in the local dailies. Parents may then collect the admission form from the school on any working day between 10.00 AM and 3:00 PM. The age for seeking admission starts from 2 years for Pre-KG and 3 years for LKG.
Parents must submit the completed admissions form along with the required documents and the application fee for the process to move forward.
