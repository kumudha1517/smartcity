Alpha International School

Alpha International School, one of the top schools in Chennai, was founded in 2015, and managed by the Alpha Educational Society. The pedagogy at Alpha International School is meant to foster a progressive, student-centric approach, where teaching is linked with a dynamic and varied curriculum. The school follows the CBSE curricula with international best practices in teaching and learning. This provides opportunities for pupils to go beyond the confines of a traditional curriculum. This is one of the best CBSE schools in Chennai.

Facilities

The school has facilities like smart classrooms, a library, a computer lab, a science lab, a math lab, and a playground.
