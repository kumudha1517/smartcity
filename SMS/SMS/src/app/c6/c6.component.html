National Public School

National Public School, one of the best schools in Chennai, was founded in 1970 and the Gopalapuram district is home to it. The Central Board of Secondary Education has accorded NPS Gopalapuram the status of permanent affiliation. Its stellar performance in the classroom and extracurricular activities, such as the personality development program, has served as a yardstick for measuring its success. It is one of the best CBSE schools in Chennai.
