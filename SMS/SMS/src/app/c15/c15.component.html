Chettinad Sarvalokaa Education

Chettinad Sarvalokaa Education, one of the best schools in Chennai, established in 2017, is an international school located on a 10-acre campus with cutting-edge amenities in the heart of the verdant 108-acre Chettinad Health City in OMR Kelambakkam, Chennai. Sarvalokaa is a residential day and boarding school that offers the Cambridge (IGCSE) curriculum. The Cambridge Assessment Group is the parent organization. It is one of the best igcse schools in Chennai.

Facilities

The school has facilities like a library, a computer lab, a science lab, a math lab, a music room, an art room, a dance room, and a playground.

Location

Chettinad – Sarvalokaa Education, International School, Inside Chettinad Health City, Rajiv Gandhi Salai, Old Mamallapuram Road, Kelambakkam, Tamil Nadu 603103

Curriculum

Cambridge (IGCSE) is offered at Chettinad – Sarvalokaa Education, International School. Young people who follow this program grow to be self-assured, accountable, introspective, creative, and actively involved in their learning. As stated in the introductory line, the curriculum is structured to let students easily progress toward IGCSE, O Level, A Level, and AS Level. This is one of the best schools in Kelambakkam.
