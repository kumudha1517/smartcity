About Loyola College:

Loyola Institute of Business Administration (LIBA) Chennai is a private business school, established in 1979. The school is approved by the AICTE and recognised by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). It is one of the best management colleges in India and has been ranked 9 for Private MBA B Schools by Outlook 2021. LIBA Chennai has also earned global accreditation from SAQS and AACSB. LIBA Chennai offers a two-year Full-time flagship PGDM course, approved by AICTE. The School also offers other programs such as Part-time PGDM, PhD. and one-year executive diploma programs.

Loyola College Courses & Fees:

Loyola College offers courses at undergraduate and postgraduate levels in various streams. Loyola College offers six programmes: B.Arch, M.Arch, M.Arch Landscape, M.Plan, M.B.A Business Analytics, and M.B.A General Management. Except for M.B.A Business Analytics and M.B.A General Management, all programmes at Loyola College are full-time. The fee for undergraduate and postgraduate programmes at Loyola College varies depending on the degree level and course duration.
