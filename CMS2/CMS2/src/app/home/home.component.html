1.IIT Madras - Indian Institute of Technology

Identified as an ‘Institute of Eminence', the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT Madras or IITM) was established in 1959 as a public technical and research university. It is the third IIT be set up by the Government of India and is autonomous in nature. Located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, the IIT Madras campus is home to India’s first university-based research park- IITMR. IIT Madras has been consistently ranked 1 by the NIRF Rankings in 2022 for the fourth time in a row. Internationally, IIT Madras was ranked 255 in the QS World University Rankings of 2022. IIT Madras, through its 16 departments, 4 national and 10 institute research centres offer more than 100 full-time/ part-time/ online courses at UG, PG, and doctoral levels across the Engineering, Science, Humanities, and Management.
