About SSN College of Engineering:

SSN College of Engineering (SSNCE) is a part of SSN institutions affiliated with Anna University. The institute is accredited by NBA and with A++ Grade by NAAC. Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar College of Engineering is approved by AICTE and UGC. SSN College Chennai, through its 12 departments, offers UG and PG courses to students across the Engineering stream. SSN College of Engineering has two sister institutes, namely the School of Management and the School of Advanced Career Education. SSN Engineering College is ranked 48 by NIRF 2022 for BTech.

SSN College of Engineering Courses & Fees:

SSN College of Engineering offers courses at undergraduate and postgraduate levels in various streams. Anna University offers six programmes: B.Arch, M.Arch, M.Arch Landscape, M.Plan, M.B.A Business Analytics, and M.B.A General Management. Except for M.B.A Business Analytics and M.B.A General Management, all programmes at Anna University are full-time. The fee for undergraduate and postgraduate programmes at Anna University varies depending on the degree level and course duration.

SSN College of Engineering Scholarships:

Students can apply for SSN College of Engineeringy scholarships through the government of India's official National Scholarship Portal. Students who want to apply for any of the scholarships must first check the eligibility requirements for the specific scholarship. Some Anna University scholarships include the Central Sector Scholarship Scheme for Top Class Education for SC Students, the Begum Hazrat Mahal National Scholarship, Scholarships for Top Class Education for Students with Disabilities, and others.

SSN College of Engineering Placements:

SSN College of Engineering placement drive is coordinated by the Centre for University-Industry Collaboration (CUIC). The centre cell also organises campus placements, industrial training, visits, and projects for students. Final-year students are placed by the Anna University Centre for University-Industry Collaboration. The cell provides soft skill development and competency building programmes to students in order to increase their employability. Anna University CUIC also works with industry leaders to provide contests, hackathons, and scholarships based on skill level.

SSN College of Engineering Facilities:

SSN College of Engineering provides numerous infrastructural facilities to assist students in having a stress-free learning environment. Anna University's facilities include a library, auditorium, laboratories, canteen, hostel, sports, and more. There are separate hostels for boys and girls on the Anna University campus. On campus, there are cooperative, departmental, and shoe stores. The cooperative store meets stationery needs, while department stores provide basic convenience. Barber, tailoring, and shoe shops are available for grooming. There are also STD/ISD booths available. Anna University also offers medical services to students, faculty, and staff.
