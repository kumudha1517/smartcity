About Sathyabama University:

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology, Chennai was established under Section 3 of the UGC Act 1956. The university is recognised by UGC and is considered a deemed-to-be university. The university is approved by AICTE, accredited by NAAC with an A++ grade and has been ranked 51st in the NIRF university ranking in the year 2023. The university offers a total of 89 courses at diploma, doctoral, undergraduate and postgraduate levels. The university offers various facilities for the students and has a well-developed infrastructure. The university provides scholarships to the students and provides placement opportunities for students in reputed companies.

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology Courses:

Sathyabama University offers a total of 89 courses out of which 1 is a diploma course, 56 are undergraduate courses, 30 are postgraduate courses and 2 are doctoral courses. The duration of these courses ranges from 2 to 6 years. Candidates can check the fee structure and eligibility criteria of these courses through the official website of the university.

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology Scholarships:

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology offers scholarships to students who require financial support. These students have low annual family incomes and good academic records. Candidates can check the eligibility criteria and apply for these scholarships through the official website of the university.

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology Placements:

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology Chennai offers placements to its students in companies from various different sectors. The university prepares its students for placements by providing training and conducting workshops. The university also provides internships to the students to enhance their practical learning.

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology Facilities:

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology offers various facilities for the students which include hostel facilities, sports facilities, medical facilities, research facilities and transport facilities. The infrastructure of the campus is environmentally friendly and is surrounded with lots of greenery. The infrastructure of the campus includes Library, Auditorium, Conference Halls, Sathyabama Centre for Advanced Studies, Child Care Centre, Bank, ATM, Classrooms and Dining Halls.

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology Location:

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology is located on Rajiv Gandhi Salai which was earlier known as Old Mahabalipuram Road in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The nearest bus stop to the university is Sathyabama University Bus Stop which is 60m away. The nearest railway station to the university is Tambaram Railway Station which is 18 km away. The nearest airport to the university is Chennai International Airport which is 21 km away. Candidates can take a cab, auto or bus to reach the university from railway station, bus stop and airport.
