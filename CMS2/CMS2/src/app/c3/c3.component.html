About SRM University:

SRM University (SRMIST) admission 2023 is open for all UG and PG programs. SRMJEEE 2023 Phase 2 result has been announced for BTech admissions. The choice filling for SRMJEEE 2023 Phase 2 Counselling will be done from June 17 to June 19. The allotment and registration for candidates selected in the SRMJEEE Phase 2 Counselling 2023 will be done between June 23 and June 27. Applications are also open for SRMJEEE Phase 3 and the last date to apply is June 26, 2023. Also, SRMJEEE PG 2023 Phase 2 applications for MTech admissions are open till June 29 and the exam will be conducted on July 2, 2023. Furthermore, the last date to apply for SRMJEEM 2023 Phase 4 application is June 19 and the exam will be held on June 24, 2023.

SRM University Courses & Fees:

SRM University offers courses at undergraduate and postgraduate levels in various streams. SRM University offers six programmes: B.Arch, M.Arch, M.Arch Landscape, M.Plan, M.B.A Business Analytics, and M.B.A General Management. Except for M.B.A Business Analytics and M.B.A General Management, all programmes at Anna University are full-time. The fee for undergraduate and postgraduate programmes at Anna University varies depending on the degree level and course duration.

SRM University Scholarships:

Merit Scholarship: Fee waiver of 50% for students with above 95% in CBSE or in State Board examinations. Founder's Scholarship: Full waiver on books, tuition, hostels & mess, plus a stipend of Rs. 1,000 per month to top state, JEE, SRMEEE & AIEEE rankers, sportspersons and socio-economic disdained. Merit-cum-means Scholarship: 25% to 100% tuition fee waiver is provided to students who are economically challenged based on their performance in the qualifying examination. Scholarships for special merits: Scholarship with tuition fee waivered up to 100% is offered to some of the students owning exemplary skills in literary debating, fine arts, and others.

SRM University Facilities:

  • Infrastructure: Over 600 acres replete with a variety of facilities, State-of-the-art labs, libraries, Wi-Fi, knowledge center, 4000 capacity AC auditorium, 100 online smart classrooms, Hostels with premium facilities, endless convenience on campus including ATM's, bookstores, dining options, cafeterias, prayer halls, gym and more.

  • Hostels: SRM University provides a wide choice in selecting the type of rooms in a hostel to suit every budget, with the aim of providing a decent stay and the best possible learning environment.

  • Library: SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly known as SRM University) has an excellent library for the benefit of students, faculties, and researchers. As one of the best-established libraries according to International Standards, the University Library has a large collection of books covering various branches of Engineering and Technology, Medical and Health Sciences and Science and Humanities and its related fields.

  • Academic Environment: Foreign faculty, flexible and dynamic curriculum, exciting research and global connections are the features that set SRM apart. Students have a wide choice of cutting edge programs including nanotechnology, bioinformatics, genetic engineering, remote sensing and GIS, embedded systems or computer forensics to choose from. Most of these courses are offered in close collaboration with foreign universities.
