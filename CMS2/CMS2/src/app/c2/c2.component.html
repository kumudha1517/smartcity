About Anna University:

Anna University is a state university established in 1978. Anna University offers 6 courses at UG and PG levels in the Management and Business Administration, Computer Application and IT, Engineering and Architecture streams. Admission to Anna University UG programmes is based on the marks obtained in the National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA). Aspiring students must meet the Anna University eligibility criteria before they apply for admission in any desired course. The Anna University placements drive is overseen by a placement committee known as the Centre for University-Industry Collaboration (CUIC). The placement committee also provides students with internship opportunities.

Anna University Courses & Fees:

Anna University offers courses at undergraduate and postgraduate levels in various streams. Anna University offers six programmes: B.Arch, M.Arch, M.Arch Landscape, M.Plan, M.B.A Business Analytics, and M.B.A General Management. Except for M.B.A Business Analytics and M.B.A General Management, all programmes at Anna University are full-time. The fee for undergraduate and postgraduate programmes at Anna University varies depending on the degree level and course duration.

Anna University Scholarships:

Students can apply for Anna University scholarships through the government of India's official National Scholarship Portal. Students who want to apply for any of the scholarships must first check the eligibility requirements for the specific scholarship. Some Anna University scholarships include the Central Sector Scholarship Scheme for Top Class Education for SC Students, the Begum Hazrat Mahal National Scholarship, Scholarships for Top Class Education for Students with Disabilities, and others.

Anna University Placements:

Anna University's placement drive is coordinated by the Centre for University-Industry Collaboration (CUIC). The centre cell also organises campus placements, industrial training, visits, and projects for students. Final-year students are placed by the Anna University Centre for University-Industry Collaboration. The cell provides soft skill development and competency building programmes to students in order to increase their employability. Anna University CUIC also works with industry leaders to provide contests, hackathons, and scholarships based on skill level.

Anna University Facilities:

Anna University provides numerous infrastructural facilities to assist students in having a stress-free learning environment. Anna University's facilities include a library, auditorium, laboratories, canteen, hostel, sports, and more. There are separate hostels for boys and girls on the Anna University campus. On campus, there are cooperative, departmental, and shoe stores. The cooperative store meets stationery needs, while department stores provide basic convenience. Barber, tailoring, and shoe shops are available for grooming. There are also STD/ISD booths available. Anna University also offers medical services to students, faculty, and staff.

Anna University Location:

Anna University is located at 12, Sardar Patel Rd, Anna University, Guindy, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The nearest airport to the university is Chennai International Airport. It is located 13 km away from the university campus. With a distance of 1.5 km, Kasthurba Nagar Railway Station is the closest railway station to Anna University. The university is located at a distance of 850 m away from the Anna University Bus Stop.
